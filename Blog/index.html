Verizon App Challenge

The problem with highschool sports apps is that they don't show ALL the information that you're looking for. Most of the time they only show the score, and when it comes to player stats they don't matchup to how the player actually plays. To fix these issues with WESPN we will be adding a play by play and streaming feature to better allow people that aren't able to make it to the game to keep up and still be able to know what is going on. Also we'll be adding player stats but make them be accurate by doing it ourselves and not allowing the players to "fib" them. We could improve our video by adding in more comments from people who have "used" the app to show others that the app actually does work. Currently we have a player by player, streaming, and allowing the players to make accounts and sync their accounts to the player stats. I'd like to add more features similar to instagram with the layout and accessability. I think the biggest challenge we faced is naming. Next time we'll be a bit more prepared with information to make it easier for us to think of a title.
